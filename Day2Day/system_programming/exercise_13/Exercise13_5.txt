1. Identify the character specified by the array index, given the following declaration:

	char array[10] = "abcdefghi";

	a. array[2]

	Answer: c

	b. array[0]

	Answer: a

	c. array[7]

	Answer: h

	d. array[3]

	Answer: d

	e. array[9]

	Answer: '\0'

2. Identify the character pointed to by the *ptr:

	char array[10] = "0123456789";
	char *ptr;

	ptr = &array[7];

	*ptr is ?

	Answer: 7
